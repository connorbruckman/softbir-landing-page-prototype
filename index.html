An all-encompassing EHR system for your practice.

ApkaMD is a telemedicine platform that allows patients to consult with licensed healthcare professionals via video, phone, or messaging. Patients can book appointments with doctors, therapists, and specialists in various fields, and receive diagnoses, prescriptions, and referrals.

Improving patient outcomes, reducing healthcare costs.

NowRPM is a remote patient monitoring platform that helps healthcare providers monitor their patients' health in real-time. Patients can use the NowRPM app to measure and track their vital signs such as blood pressure, heart rate, and oxygen levels, and share the data with their healthcare providers.
